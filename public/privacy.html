2. Data We Collect

Free Version

  • No user data is collected or stored.

  • Because we do not track users, we do not require a cookie or consent banner.

Paid Version

  • Authentication Data: Credentials (secured using industry-standard encryption techniques, such as stateless JWT tokens).

Technical & Debugging Data

  • We do not store IP addresses, logs, or metadata.
  • We use error tracking software to monitor system stability, but no personally identifiable information is collected or stored.
  • We use analytics software, but no personally identifiable information is collected or stored.

We collect user data only to:

  • Provide core service functionality (e.g., game progress, reporting, leaderboard access).

  • Maintain the security and stability of the Service.

We do not sell user data or use it for marketing purposes. Future features may use collected data only for service improvements and will be communicated transparently.

4. Data Sharing & Third-Party Services

We do not sell or share user data unless required for core service operations or by law. Data may be shared in the following cases:

  • Hosting & Infrastructure: User data is stored with a cloud hosting provider for service functionality.
  • Legal Requirements: We may comply with legally binding requests but will resist unauthorized access requests to the fullest extent permitted by law.

We do not share user data with third parties for commercial purposes.

5. Where We Store & Process Data

8. Security & Data Protection

  • Data is encrypted in transit and at rest.

This allows for greater community scrutiny, promotes trust and security, and reduces the risk of hidden data tracking.
